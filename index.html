About LifeLink

Connecting Donors and Patients

LifeLink is a blood donation platform designed to make it easier for people in need of blood to connect with willing donors. Our goal is to reduce the time it takes to find a matching donor and bring hope to patients and their families.

As a donor, you can create an account, share your blood type and location, and be notified when someone nearby needs your help. As a requester, you can submit a blood request with important details like blood type, urgency, hospital, and contact information.

How the Website Will Work

Right now, the website focuses on a smooth front-end experience with clear forms for registration, login, and blood requests. In the next step, a backend (for example, a Node.js + Express API) will be connected so that all requests and registrations are securely saved in a database.

Once the backend is connected, admins and donors will be able to see active requests, accept them, and mark them as fulfilled. This future integration will turn LifeLink into a complete real-world blood donation management system.
